# 🌸 SheCare – Women Empowerment Platform

## 📌 Project Overview

**SheCare** is a web platform designed to support and empower women at every stage of life.
The platform focuses on providing awareness, resources, and opportunities related to **women’s health, education, employment, and government welfare schemes**.

The goal of this project is to create a centralized platform where women can easily access helpful information, support systems, and career opportunities.

This project was developed as part of a **Women’s Day initiative**, aiming to promote equality, awareness, and empowerment.

---

# 🎯 Objectives

* Promote **women empowerment and awareness**
* Provide **health guidance and menstrual health tracking**
* Share information about **government schemes for women**
* Encourage **financial independence through skill-based opportunities**
* Improve **access to safety resources and emergency support**

---

# 🌍 Key Features

## 🏠 Home Page

* Women empowerment message
* Inspirational quotes
* Statistics about women in India
* Awareness about education and employment

---

## 🩺 Women Health Section

This section focuses on improving health awareness among women.

Features include:

* Menstrual cycle awareness
* Pregnancy and maternal health information
* Nutrition guidance
* Mental health awareness

---

## 💼 Opportunities for Women

Provides ideas for women to earn income through various activities.

Examples:

* Home Tiffin Business
* Tailoring and Fashion Designing
* Handmade Products
* Online Teaching
* Freelancing Opportunities

---

## 🏛 Government Schemes

Information about important government initiatives supporting women.

Examples:

* Beti Bachao Beti Padhao
* Sukanya Samriddhi Yojana
* Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana Yojana
* Ujjwala Yojana
* Mahila E-Haat
* STEP Skill Development Scheme

Each scheme includes a **direct link to official government websites**.

---

## 🚨 Women Safety Section

Provides important emergency resources for women.

Emergency contacts include:

* Women Helpline: **1091**
* National Women Helpline: **181**
* Police: **100**
* Ambulance: **108**

---

## 👩 Profile Section

Allows women to manage personal information.

Details include:

* Name
* Age
* Phone number
* Address
* Marital status
* Skills and interests

# 📊 Women Empowerment Facts

* Female literacy rate in India is approximately **70%**
* Women workforce participation is around **25%**
* Many women delay healthcare due to family responsibilities

This platform aims to encourage women to **prioritize education, health, and independence**.
