    Safety While Traveling

    • Always share your live location with at least two family members.
    • Keep emergency speed dials ready on your phone (usually Power button 3-5 times).
    • Trust your instincts—if a place feels unsafe, leave immediately.
    • Stay in well-lit, crowded areas during night travel.
